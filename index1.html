What exactly are we doing here?? Well keep on reading and you'll see!

Our objective

To understand the change in popularity in genres throughout the years beginning with two kaggle sets, which include a data set from Pitchfork and another dataset from Spotify.

The questions we were trying to answer

Who were the best selling artists

What were the least popular songs?

What were the one hit wonders?

What were the best selling genres?

What were the genre/decade defining artists of the 2000's?
